Challenges & How I Tackled Them

✔ Balancing AI & Human Expertise

Users often find AI suggestions impersonal, but relying solely on human mentors could lead to inconsistency.

Solution: Designed an interface where AI generates insights, and human mentors provide validation and personalized recommendations, creating a hybrid approach.

✔ Building Trust in AI-Generated Insights

Students and parents were hesitant to rely on AI-driven career suggestions.

Solution: Added transparency by showcasing reasoning behind AI recommendations, explaining career paths, and enabling mentor feedback.

✔ Simplifying Complex Career Data

Too much information can overwhelm students rather than guide them.

Solution: Created structured dashboards with visual progress tracking, step-by-step career roadmaps, and bite-sized insights to keep engagement high.

Final Outcome

Edvdo was built from the ground up to bridge the gap between AI-driven career recommendations and human mentorship. The platform successfully integrates smart automation with expert insights, creating a personalized, efficient, and accessible career guidance experience.
